Podcast producers

If you are a producer/creator of a podcast or other audio stories and you have speech tracks that you would be willing to share with us, please let us know! Short tracks are fine. Email maneesh at cs.berkeley.edu and srubin at cs.berkeley.edu. In return, we'll get your speech track set up to be edited in our system.
